About Me

Source: Unsplash

I'm Sarah Mitchell, and I'm not a travel blogger or influencer. I'm someone who found herself regularly visiting Dubai Pool and decided to document the reality of the experience.

Why I Started This Site

I moved to Dubai in early 2024 for work, and like many newcomers, I was exploring what the city had to offer. Dubai Pool kept coming up in recommendations—friends mentioned it, I saw it on social media, and it seemed like a must-visit experience.

My first visit was... interesting. It wasn't bad, but it wasn't what I expected based on what I'd seen online. The photos showed an almost empty, serene paradise. The reality was different—crowded, busy, but still enjoyable if you knew what to expect.

I went back a few times, trying different times of day, different days of the week, and I started noticing patterns. What worked, what didn't, what was worth the money, what wasn't. I found myself giving advice to friends who were planning visits, and I realized there wasn't a resource that gave honest, practical information from a regular visitor's perspective.

So I decided to create one.

My Approach

These articles aren't sponsored content or marketing material. I pay for my own visits, I experience the facility as a regular customer, and I write about what actually happens. No fluff, no exaggeration, just honest observations.

I focus on practical information that actually helps when you're planning a visit or trying to understand what to expect. What time to arrive, what to bring, what the facilities are really like, what works and what doesn't.

I'm not trying to sell you on the experience or talk you out of it. I'm just sharing what I've learned from multiple visits, so you can make informed decisions and have realistic expectations.

What You'll Find Here

This site contains detailed accounts of my experiences at Dubai Pool, organized into three main articles:

  • First Visit Experience: What actually happens when you visit for the first time, from check-in to navigating the facility
  • Facilities & Amenities: A practical guide to what the facilities are really like, tested through multiple visits
  • Tips & Recommendations: Specific advice on timing, what to bring, and how to make the most of your visit

All of this is written from a user's perspective, focusing on information that's actually useful rather than generic travel advice.

My Background

I work in marketing and communications, which means I understand how experiences are presented versus how they actually are. I've lived in several cities around the world, and I've learned that the best way to understand a place is through repeated, real experiences, not curated content.

I'm not a professional writer or reviewer—I'm just someone who likes to document experiences and share useful information. If you find these articles helpful, that's the goal.

Contact Me

If you have questions, feedback, or want to share your own experiences, feel free to get in touch. I'm always interested in hearing from other visitors and learning from their experiences.

I update these articles periodically as I continue to visit and learn more about the facility. The information here is current as of my last visit, but things can change, so I'll update as needed.

Thanks for reading, and I hope these articles help you have a better experience at Dubai Pool, whatever that means for you.
